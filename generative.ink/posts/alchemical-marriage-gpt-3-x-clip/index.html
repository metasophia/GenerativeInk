CLIP responds intruigingly well to figurative prompts. I’ve come to expect not just more interesting, but more coherent results from abstract but evocative prompts like the alchemical marriage of art and artist than literal descriptions like three birds on a telephone wire. GPT-3 is an excellent composer of prompts for BigSleep because its phrases are so often rich in memetic resonance, my heuristic for the expected coherence of BigSleep yields for a given prompt.
